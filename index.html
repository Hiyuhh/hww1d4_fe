Valorant Tips:

1. Learn how to strafe shoot.
2. Master your agent and how to use your abilities.
3. Practice in the range and Deathmatch/Team Deathmatch.
4. Learn how to play and communicate with your team.
5. Learn how to take advantage of post-plant and play time.
